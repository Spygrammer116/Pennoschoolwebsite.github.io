About our school

Welcome to Pennant Hills High School, where excellence in education meets a vibrant community spirit. Nestled in the
heart of Pennant Hills, our school boasts a rich history of academic achievements, innovative learning programs, and a diverse range of extracurricular activities. Our dedicated teachers and modern facilities ensure every student is given the opportunity to flourish in a nurturing and challenging environment.

At Pennant Hills High, we pride ourselves on fostering the leaders of tomorrow, instilling in them not just knowledge but the important values of responsibility, integrity, and achievement, as well as a lifelong love of learning.
