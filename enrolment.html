Enrolment Process at Pennant Hills High School

Local enrolment area

Check your address on school finder to see if it is within our local intake area. Most schools have designated local enrolment areas. Note: local enrolment areas are subject to change.

How to enrol

If you live in our intake area and would like to apply to enrol at our school, start by submitting an online enrolment application. Online enrolment is currently only available for Australian or New Zealand citizens and permanent residents.

If you are not able to apply online or you prefer not to, download the paper application to enrol form. Please complete the form in English. translated application to enrol may help you do this.

Online enrolment can also be used for out-of-area enrolment. Please note that out-of-area enrolment procedures are subject to the departmentâ€™s enrolment policy.

Enrolment applications are finalised at the school.

For more information, visit high school enrolment.

How to enrol – Year 6 into Year 7

The Moving into Year 7 in a NSW Government school in 2024 booklet provides information about the process for enrolment in a government secondary school. This includes the Expression of Interest (EOI) form for placement in Year 7, 2024 in a NSW Government school.

Year 6 parents/caregivers complete this form to indicate their preferences for the school in which they would like to enrol their child for Year 7, 2024. You can indicate which school you would like your child to attend by completing the Expression of Interest form for your local school and/or a maximum of 3 out-of-area schools.

Parents/caregivers of Year 6 Students currently attending non-Government schools should contact Pennant Hills High School to receive the Moving into Year 7 in a NSW Government school in 2024 booklet.

If you have received an offer for placement into Year 7 next year and submitted your expression of interest online, visit the online enrolment sign in page to accept this offer and progress your application to enrol.

If you did not submit your expression of interest online, are not eligible or would prefer not to enrol online, use the application to enrol


- Expression of Interest forms must be submitted before completing the general Application to Enrol in a NSW Government School.
