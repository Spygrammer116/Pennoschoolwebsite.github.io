About Us

Welcome to Pennant Hills High School, a leading comprehensive, co-educational institution nestled in the serene suburb of Pennant Hills, Sydney, New South Wales. Just a brief 15-20 minute walk from the Pennant Hills railway station, our school is ideally situated to provide a conducive learning environment, away from the bustling city yet well-connected for ease of accessâ€‹.

At Pennant Hills High School, our ethos is rooted in a commitment to providing a high-quality, inclusive, and future-focused education. Our supportive atmosphere empowers every student to thrive academically, socially, and personally. With a student population of over 1,000 pupils and a low student-to-teacher ratio of 1 to 15, personalized attention and a nurturing educational journey are at the heart of our offering.

Our curriculum is robust, designed to foster academic excellence alongside creativity, critical thinking, and real-world skills. Students have a wide array of subjects to choose from, ranging from the sciences, mathematics, and languages, to creative and performing arts, home economics, and technology and applied studies, among others.
